Kite Surf is awesome

Kite surfing, also known as kiteboarding, is an exhilarating water sport that combines elements of surfing , windsurfing, and wakeboarding. Participants use a small surfboard or kiteboard, typically made of foam or fiberglass, and are propelled across the water by a large controllable kite. The kite is connected to the rider by lines, allowing them to harness the power of the wind to perform jumps, tricks, and maneuvers on the water surface. Kite surfing requires a combination of skill, balance, and control, as well as knowledge of wind conditions and safety protocols. It's a popular sport enjoyed by enthusiasts worldwide, offering an adrenaline-pumping experience in coastal and windy locations.
